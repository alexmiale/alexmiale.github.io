  • The Role of Social Media in the Development of Adolescents - Spring 2025

    For the final paper in my Information Ethics class, I wrote a paper about how social media has been and still is negatively affecting the next generation. I took some inspiration from Jonathon Haidt and his book titled "The Anxious Generation" which talks about a "great rewiring" as children transition into a phone and screen-based childhood. I explain how adolescents are at increased risk on social media and how this affects their relationships and view of themselves. Then, I use ethical theory to defend my argument and proposed policy to combat this issue. I also respond to an objection of my argument before concluding the essay. I feel a special connection to this paper because I have seen the harm of social media on adolescents firsthand, as I am apart of the first generation to use it. This paper was more than a final grade; it was a passion piece as much as it was technical. This essay is a demonstration of my desire to help those who cannot help themselves, and why I am interested in pursuing a degree and career in public policy.
